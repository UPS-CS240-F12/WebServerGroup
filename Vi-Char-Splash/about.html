About

Choose your side

  • Three players control a single robot, who is struggling to stay alive in a deadly arena.
  • Up to four mobile phone players are working together to destroy you.
  • Choose your team and work together to achieve victory!

Unique control schemes

  • Three players must work together to control a single character using a Microsoft Kinect.
  • Mobile Phones interact with the game world using Augmented Reality.
  • Spectators can influence the game via a Twitter-based voting system.

Follow the action

  • Watch the current game status on the Vi-Char website.
  • View the leaderboard to see who got the highest scores.
  • Download the Android app to take part in the action!
